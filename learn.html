Goal of the game:

  • The goal of playing BlackJacks is to beat the dealer by getting as close to 21 as possible without going over.

How the game is played:

  • Press the Deal button to start a new round.
  • You will recieve two cards, both facing up.
  • The dealer will also recieve two cards, though one will face up and the other down.
  • You will then select one of the four buttons below based on the dealers and your cards.

What each button does:

  • Hit — This button prompts another card to be added to your card count.
  • Stand — This button prompts you to stay at your card count and end your turn.
  • Double — This button prompts you to double the amount of money that you want to bet on your hand, and will only give you one card and then will end your turn.
  • Split — This button prompts you to split the two cards that you were given if they are the same number, you are then given two seperate hands to play.

Understanding the chips:

  • $5 - This prompts you to bet 5 dollars on your hand.
  • $10 - This prompts you to bet 10 dollars on your hand.
  • $25 - This prompts you to bet 25 dollars on your hand.
  • $100 - This prompts you to bet 100 dollars on your hand.

Winning or Losing:

  • If your card count is higher than the dealers and not over 21, then you win!
  • If your card count goes over 21, then you lose your bet and the dealer wins automatically.
  • If the dealers card count goes over 21, or you bust, then you win automatically.

Tips you should follow:

  • Always stand on 17 or higher.
  • Split Aces, 8s, 10s, or face cards whenever possible.
  • Enjoy the game!
